About

TrackYourTot turns your device into an easy to use and reliable Baby Monitor. Keep one device with your baby and watch live video with crystal clear audio on your other devices. Chat with other Moms in the community.

    How to get started?

    1. Create a room

    Create a room for you and your little one.

    2. Copy & share the link

    Copy the link to the room and send it on e-mail, chat, SMS etc. to your baby's device

    3. Watch your baby!

    Once they click the link, they will appear in the room with you!
